### The avatar crossing (the interesting bit)

The agent avatar can be an image, an emoji, or an **animal** — and the animal case needs the app's `AnimalAvatar` (an onboarding component). So the avatar crosses into the shared layer **as a slot, not as data**:

- The app's `resolveComposerAgentPresentation` (unchanged) normalizes each agent into `{ displayName, description, avatar-union }`.
- A thin **app-side** `ComposerAgentAvatar` adapter renders the union to a `ReactNode` (`image` -> `<img>`, `animal` -> `AnimalAvatar`, `emoji` -> `<span>`).
- `AgentPickerOption` carries `avatar: ReactNode`; chat-ui's `AgentPicker` just places it.
