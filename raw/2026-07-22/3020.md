## Summary

Implements the approved design (spec + plan merged in #3016). Pending interactive card-kit cards (`buttons` / `select`) now **float above the live input composer** instead of being buried inline in the scrolling message list; the composer stays usable (non-blocking). Once a card is answered, the server-produced completion **`banner`** renders inline as a permanent, **user-side** receipt. Replay and the other live surfaces are unchanged.

This reverses the old behavior where an unresolved card **hid the whole composer** while the buttons sat up in the bubble.

App-side only — `@zooclaw/chat-ui` is not touched.

### Approach

- **Pure selectors** (`chat/lib/interactive-card-float.ts`): `selectFloatingInteractiveCards` (which pending cards to float, grouped by post) + `selectInlineInteractiveCards` (which cards render inline per message).
- **`FloatingInteractiveCards`** host stacks pending cards above the composer, reusing the existing app `InteractiveCards` wrapper unchanged (same `doPostAction` transport / replay guard / toast).
