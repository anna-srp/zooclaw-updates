## Summary

Backend slice of **ECA-1269** (engine agent session threads). Engine agents couldn't have thread-per-session chat: the conversation list/create/rename surface only existed as computer-addressed routes (`/computers/{cid}/agents/{aid}/conversations*`), and the only engine "sessions" route on the `/agents` group read the **wrong source** — controld's engine `session_store` (agent-side transcript), not the user-visible Mattermost conversation.

This adds **workspace-keyed conversation routes** on the unified `/agents` group, serving **both runtimes**, and removes the wrong-source route.

Linear: https://linear.app/srpone/issue/ECA-1269
Plan: `docs/superpowers/plans/2026-07-21-eca-1269-engine-session-threads.md`
Follow-up: the frontend workspace-keyed thread route (`/chat/{workspaceId}/sessions/{sessionId}`) stacks on this as Slice 2.
