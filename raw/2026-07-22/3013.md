## Summary

ECA-1288 Add-menu slice (**Approach Y**): migrates the composer Add-menu dropdown tree — `ComposerAddMenu` **and** the nested `ComposerSkillsMenu` (Skills submenu) — from the app-local `@/components/ds` primitives to `@zooclaw/design-system`, **in place**. This puts the composer's whole dropdown tree on the shared design system and finally brings the Skills submenu along.

### Why in-place, not a chat-ui extraction

The Add menu is ~90% orchestration (6 open states, the recent-assets react-query with `menuSession` / `requestGeneration` / `assetIdentity` race guards, the `Cmd/Ctrl+U` shortcut, and the `dialogCloseFocusRef` / `onCloseAutoFocus` / `flushSync` focus flow) wrapped around a nested Radix dropdown tree. Extracting that presentation to chat-ui would need a large, awkward props surface and would split the race/focus guards across the package boundary. Like the thread/surface that's genuinely dep-blocked, the Add menu is inherently an **orchestration** component — the right move is to put its dropdown on the shared DS, not to force it into the package.
