## Summary

ECA-1288 Skills slice (dialog only): moves the **Skill Store dialog** presentation into `@zooclaw/chat-ui` as a new `SkillStoreDialog` (composing `@zooclaw/design-system` primitives), and reduces the app's `ComposerSkillMarketDialog` to a **thin data-container** that feeds it.

### Why dialog-only (scoping note)

The Skills surface is two pieces with very different coupling:

- The **Skills submenu** (`ComposerSkillsMenu`) is a `DropdownMenuSub` nested inside `ComposerAddMenu`, which uses the **app-local `@/components/ds`** dropdown. A chat-ui submenu would use `@zooclaw/design-system`'s dropdown — a *different Radix instance*, which won't share menu context when nested. So the submenu is welded to the Add menu and **moves with the Add-menu slice** (last). It is untouched here.
- The **Skill Store dialog** is a standalone `Dialog` (portal-based, already on `@zooclaw/design-system`), so it extracts cleanly now — exactly like `ModelPicker`/`AgentPicker`, just a dialog.
